# **App Name**: Amisamishka Franchise Launchpad

## Core Features:

- Franchise Model Display: Showcase the 3 franchise models with details like investment range, ROI, space required, target audience, and key benefits in a card-based layout.
- Earning Potential Visualizer: Present visual examples of potential income with a breakdown of monthly revenue vs. expenses to motivate potential franchisees. Includes downloadable franchise brochure (PDF).
- Social Media Integration: Integrate social media feeds (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn) and a floating WhatsApp button for direct inquiries.
- Lead Capture Form: Implement a simple inquiry form to collect leads, capturing name, phone, email, city, and investment budget, integrated to a backend.
- Earnings Projection Assistant: AI powered "tool" to generate personalized earning projections based on user input of location and market conditions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant orange (#FFA500) to convey energy and a food-oriented theme.
- Background color: Light orange (#FFF3E0), a desaturated tint of the primary, creates a bright and welcoming feel.
- Accent color: Yellow-orange (#FFC34D) to draw attention to important elements, like the CTA buttons.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', sans-serif, known for a contemporary and fashionable vibe.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a versatile and humanist sans-serif suitable for larger bodies of text.
- Use food-themed icons that complement the franchise models, making the overall feel funky but premium.
- Incorporate subtle scroll animations and hover effects to enhance user engagement without compromising load times.
